Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Velo is a minimal study timer and assignment tracker built for students. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Velo stores all your assignments, focus sessions, and preferences locally in your browser using Local Storage. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views and basic interactions. This helps us understand how the app is used and improve it. Google Analytics may use cookies. You can opt out via the cookie consent banner.

Cookies

Velo itself does not set cookies. However, third-party services like Google Analytics may use cookies for anonymous analytics. You can decline analytics cookies when prompted.

Error Tracking

We use Sentry to capture JavaScript errors and crashes. Error reports may include technical information such as browser type, operating system, and stack traces. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Data Storage

  • All user data (tasks, timer sessions, settings) is stored in your browser's Local Storage.
  • No data is sent to any server.
  • Clearing your browser data will erase all Velo data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Sentry — error and crash reporting

Children's Privacy

Velo does not knowingly collect any personal data from children or any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our GitHub repository.
